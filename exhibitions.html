Sience vs. Covid 19

Hva er et virus? Hvordan fungerer ansiktsmasker? Hvordan blir en vaksine til? Og hvordan jobber forskere for å bekjempe pandemien? Besøk vår nyeste utstilling og bli med på bekjempelsen av Covid 19

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Powerfull Physics

What is pressure? What is energy? What is weight? And what is speed? Get to know Newton’s and his laws and experience the physics of your body in our carousel! Take control of a robbot using air pressure! And get light in the light bulbs in our electrical lab

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by. Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Your body

How do we hear? How can we see? How does the brain work? What are thoughts and feelings? Your body is a world of mysteries. Get to know the organs, the skeleton, the blood and how it all fits together. Here you can measure your blood sugar, look at cells under a microscope and go for a walk through the digestive system.

Chemestry

Something can be mixed. Some bubbles. Something disappears. Some are hard and some are fluid. And something says BANG! Join and explore the world of chemistry in our chemistry lab.

Ecology

As we learn more and more of the impact humans are having on the planet, the more important it becomes that scientists explore how to create a sustainable future not just for humans but for the whole planet. In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution. But we also focus on the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter future.
